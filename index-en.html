About Us

ArrivedYet is a transit information app designed for Hong Kong residents and tourists, committed to providing the most accurate and timely public transportation information.

Our goal is to use technology innovation to help every user easily access public transit information and optimize their travel experience.

The data used by the app comes from public information provided by the MTR Corporation and is continuously updated to maintain accuracy.

Technical Information

  • Data Source: MTR Real-time Train Service Information API
  • Supported Platform: Android 11.0+
  • Future Updates: Bus and minibus integration, route planning features
